Things I like and things I do

Nature

I love nature, and thanks to the hashtags #lichensubscribe and #mosstodon on mastodon I have started enjoying the small pockets of nature around me again. I have loved animals my entire life, and have been vegan because of that for a while now.

Books, Podcasts, etc.

When I get the time to read books for fun I sometimes remember to track what I am reading at Fnessa@bookwyrm.social, which is also on the Fediverse!

Music

I have been playing violin for a few years now, but sadly I do not have the time and energy to practice as much as I want to. Because of my own playing I have started to love Western classical music, especially from the romantic or baroque eras. Some of my favourite pieces in no particular order are:

  • Sibelius Violin Concerto
  • Rachmaninov Piano Concerto No. 2
  • Tchaikovsky Symphony No. 6
  • Wieniawski Violin Concerto No. 1
  • Anything by J. S. Bach really, but especially the Chaconne of his Partita for Violin No. 2
But I do not exclusively listen to classical music, and am able to enjoy many different genres of music, and am always looking for something new to listen to.
